Project Overview

Snow of War delves into the emotional and societal burdens placed on women during wartime. This project explores themes of guilt, resilience, and societal expectations, using visual storytelling to evoke empathy and reflection.

Key Themes

  • Discrimination faced by women during war.
  • The emotional toll of societal responsibilities.
  • Exploring the fragility and strength of the human spirit.
